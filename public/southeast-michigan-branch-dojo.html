Southeast Michigan - Branch Dojo

The Southeast Michigan Branch dojo holds class at the Macomb Township Parks & Recreation Center. Classes are ran on a session basis, typically 6-7 weeks in length.

To sign up for classes, please contact Macomb Township Park & Recreation at 992-2900 or online at gomtpr.org. Class is usually on break during August and the last half of Decemeber and early January. After signing up, please contact the Southeast Michigan dojo so we can prepare beginner materials for your first class!
