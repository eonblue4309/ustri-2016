Training Information

Michigan Honbu Dojo - East Lansing, Michigan

The Southeast Michigan Branch dojo holds class at Independence Township Parks and Recreation's Bay Court Park facility. Classes are ran on a monthly schedule.

To sign up for classes, please contact Independence Twp. Park & Rec at 625-8223 or online at itpr.org. Class is usually on break during August and the last half of Decemeber. After contacting Parks & Rec, please contact the Southeast Michigan dojo so we can prepare beginner materials for your first class!

Training Schedule

Wednesday, 7:00pm - 8:30pm (Mixed Level)

If there are five Wednesdays in a month, there will be no class on the fifth Wednesday.
